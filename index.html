He started working in research shortly after his Freshman year of Undergraduate studies at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and is continuing his education by pursuing his Masters in Information Science. LoFi Beats

I learned that you can generate a lofi character from the LoFi Beats to Study and Relax To and that there's merchandise and you can purchase and can access their links to the playlists on their website

I've been listening to a ton of LoFi music while working on my website. So far, I've been able to develop this home page with links to a few of my social medias and my current resume, but eventually I'm hoping to be able to add more "blog-like" components and/or links to more direct work that would help me showcase my abilities. I also admit that I had a lot of help from the w3schools template on "how to build a website," but through using that resource, I've learned more about html structures.

The good news is that I'm becoming more confident with how to use resources like w3schools and even though I'm relying a bit heavily on the "div" tag, I'm able to understand more about inheritance and how it affects my project. For example, the header background and navbar each use opacity, which affects the text that I put inside them as well. Over time, I hope to change that and make it so my text pops out a bit more from the background itself.

In fact, I've been looking at different websites and trying to take a look at how they're designed a bit more purposefully. I'm not sure I'll every want to do something as overtly stylized as this example page on SquareSpace. That said, I tend to spend a lot of my free time thinking about hobbies, which involves looking at community driven websites, and a style that I really appreciate is at this blog
