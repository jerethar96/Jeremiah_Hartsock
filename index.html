About Me

Christmas 2021

Jeremiah Hartsock is a research professional passionate about dissemination and resource accessibility. He started working in research shortly after his Freshman year of Undergraduate studies at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and is continuing his education by pursuing his Masters in Information Science. His goal is to serve as an systems architect and information coordinator - an individual who helps clinicians and researchers interpret available information and communicate effective systems analyses and information needs across industry partners and stakeholders. In his free time he enjoys listening to and exploring music music, playing tabletop games surrounded by friendly folx, and a nice cup of pour-over coffee.
