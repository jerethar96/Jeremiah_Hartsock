Example Research Studies

A look at the research studies I assisted with implementing

Helping Giving Away Psychological Science

The studies here have no names currently; however, the idea is about information dissemination. The first round involves a collection of mental health resources from clinicians

  • The Purpose

    This study works towards improving the available evidence-based psychological resources to the public. It seeks to provide a repository of credible/useful information that is not advertised content.

  • Methods

    Two methods used were volunteer sampling of mental health professionals providing resources followed by a systematic review of the accumulated materials.

  • Implementation

    My role on this study is to provide assistance in survey development, perform literature reviews in the field of information science, and to assist in the writing of an academic manuscript.

STARR NC

Although this study does not have a logo yet, this schema of our study provides a glimpse into the tools for the study.

  • The Purpose

    This study provides navigation for Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis (PrEP) care in Rural North Carolina counties. Clinics from across the State are randomized to either receive or not receive access to a PrEP Navigator - who will assist young people with finding access to care; however, both randomized arms will include access to a mobile application designed to help young people on PrEP stay current on their sexual health care.

  • Methods

    This study is clinical randomized control trial of a multilevel intervention for PrEP strategies in Rural NC STI clinics.

  • Implementation

    My role on this study will be primarily within the development of data capture systems

AllyQuest

  • The Purpose

    To provide a smart-phone application that supports HIV medication adherence among young men who have sex with men and transgender women.

  • Methods

    This study utilized Sequential Multiple Assignment Randomized Trials (SMART) adaptive intervention method. The intervention treatment is re-assessed depending on the participant needs rather than a single assignment.

  • Implementation

    My role included the creation and maintenance of electronic case report forms, authoring of reports bi-weekly and monthly, coming up with agenda items and taking notes for team meetings, recruiting and following-up with participants, and developing documentation strategies for team members.

P3

(Prepared, Protected, emPowered)

  • The Purpose

    This was a Randomized Control Trial that tested the use of a mobile app designed to improve adherence to PrEP. The app utilized social networking features, and gamification to drive participation on the platform.

  • Methods

    This study included a control arm and two intervention arms of varied dosage. An open-label 6-month longitudinal study factorial assignment.

  • Implementation

    My role relied on recruiting and following-up with participants, maintaining and improving documentation practices, troubleshooting data collection errors from the application, and logging sample collections.

HMP

(Health eMPowerment)

  • The Purpose

    This is an umbrella term for an application platform that is used for many studies within the BATLab. In my rendition of the application, I worked on HMP Stigma which was designed to provide a safe space for young men who have sex with men and transgender women to discuss sexual health and connect with people, hopefully to improve HIV care outcomes.

  • Methods

    This is a clinical trial of a mobile application with randomized parallel assignment. There were three arms in the study.

    1. Arm 1: participants given information only to view on the app
    2. Arm 2: participants given access to a community building/interactive site on the app
    3. Arm 3: participants enrolled in Arm 2 have the option to enroll peers onto the app

  • Implementation

    In this study I developed better systems of information retrieval by organizing team meetings between select staff and our industry partner who developed the HMP website and database.

    I also monitored forum activity on the app to ensure participants were engaged with the material.
