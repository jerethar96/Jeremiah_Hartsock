TableTop Games

I love tabletop games (and video games too), and part of this is the painting projects that come along with playing them. There are tons of resources out there for the actual playing and hobbying part of the games too. Two of my favorites include:

  • Can You Roll A Crit

    This UK gamer goes over how to play guides, tournament statistics, and how to enter the particular tabletop wargame I've been enjoying

  • Eons of Battle

    This hobbyist makes very fun casual content that can be helpful for people who want to dive into painting and building nerdy little miniatures
